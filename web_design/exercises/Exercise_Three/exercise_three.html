Ordered Lists

  1. Ordered/Numbered lists are used when sequencing matters.
  2. Typical ordered lists number by 1, 2, 3. We can format these to roman numerals (I,II,III), Lower Case(a,b,c), or Alphabet(A,B,C)

Unordered Lists

  • Unordered lists are used when sequencing does not matter.
  • Bulleted lists - use bullet points to organize information
  • Format these points to other images, shapes, etc.

How to cook Romen Noodles

  1. Open package
  2. DO NOT PUT IN MICROWAVE EVER
  3. Put water in pot and boil
  4. Put noodles in pot, DO NOT PUT FLAVOR PACKET IN WATER YET
  5. Watch carefully to prevent burning, they are done when the noodles are soft
  6. When done drain the water
  7. Add flavor packet and stir
  8. ENJOY!
